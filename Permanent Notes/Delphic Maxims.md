Three maxims were written on a column of the Temple of Apollo at Delphi

*Γνῶθι σεαυτόν* — Know thyself

*Μηδὲν ἄγαν*    — Nothing in excesss

*Ἐγγύα πάρα δ ἄτα* — Surety brings ruin
